There are 4 main types of recomendations algorithms in ML.

People with the same taste, like same things.
b) Content based filtering

c) Hybrid

Suggest what is viral at the moment.

3. Taxi prices: YandexGo, MyTaxi, Uber and ect.
There are 4 main factors for prediction of price.

a) Surge pricing (more orders, higher price)
b) Number of drivers (more drivers, lower price)
c) Distance and time (longer distance and/or traffic jams higher price)
d) Weather (Rain\Snow, higher price)
